Paz Vega is a well-known Spanish actress and model who started her acting career in 1997 with the show Menudo es mi padre. Thereafter, she went on to be cast in many distinct roles such as Lucía in Lucía y el s*xo (2001), Flor Moreno in Spanglish (2004), Scarlet in 10 Items or Less (2006), Giulia Carni in All Roads Lead to Rome (2015), and Carmen Delgado in Rambo: Last Blood (2019). Besides that, her acting credits include numerous other films including Sobreviviré (1999), Mine Alone (2001), Carmen (2003), The Lark Farm (2007), Not Forgotten (2009), La ignorancia de la sangre (2014), and Acts of Vengeance (2017). Over time, Paz has also worked along with the likes of well-known stars like Antonio Banderas, Adam Sandler, Morgan Freeman, Sarah Jessica Parker, Raoul Bova, Rosie Day, Sylvester Stallone, Rob Lowe, and Nicole Kidman.

Besides that, the aesthetic beautiful actress is also a well-known model who had been featured on the cover of various distinguished magazines like Vogue, Glamour, and Harper’s Bazaar. With time, she has also amassed a huge fan base with more than 200k followers on Facebook and more than 400k followers on Instagram.

    Paz Vega Facts

  • She was raised in a Catholic household in Seville.
  • Paz relocated to Los Angeles in 2008.
  • Her stage name “Vega” was taken from one of her grandmother’s.
  • After studying acting for 2 years, she moved to Madrid in order to pursue her passion for acting. She worked part-time at bars to support herself while she gave auditions.
  • Paz was 21 years old when she made her first TV show appearance in Menudo es mi padre in 1997.
  • She was 16 years old when she attended poet and playwright Federico García Lorca’s theatre production titled, La casa de Bernarda Alba. Ever since that day, Paz fell in love with the profession of acting and that was all she wanted to become when she grew up.
  • Paz Vegas was 31 years old when she had first child, 33 at the time of her 2nd, and 34 when she gave birth to her 3rd child.
  • During her younger days, Vega would stay up after lights out time watching black and white films through a small gap in the door.
  • She became the first actress to be nominated twice for the prestigious Goya Award in 2001, out of which, she won the “Best breakthrough actress performance” category.
  • Paz had gone through the compulsory learning program until the age of 16.
  • The 2002 film Talk to Her, which featured her as Amparo won more than 30 international awards. A few of which also included an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award, and 7 Goya Award nominations.
  • She made her USA debut as Flor Moreno alongside actor Adam Sandler, Téa Leoni, Cloris Leachman in Spanglish in 2004. At the time of the shooting, Paz could neither speak nor understand English.
  • Paz was featured in singer and composer Alejandro Sanz‘s music video for A la primera persona in 2006.
  • In 2008, Paz was ranked at #85 on the Maxim’s 2008 “Hot 100” list.
  • Her younger sister Sara is a noted flamenco dancer and actress. Her acting credits include her appearance in various films including Bienvenidos al Lolita (2014), José Mota presenta (2015), and Anclados (2015).
  • Paz’s father was a bullfighter in the past, whereas, her mother is a homemaker.
  • When she’s not busy shooting, she spends her time modeling and is signed to some of the top agencies like IMG Models and One Management.
  • In 2010, Paz told Vogue, that she did not want to marry an actor.
  • Actress Penélope Cruz who had previously been the brand ambassador of L’Oreal Spain was replaced by Paz in May 2011. She is also often compared to the beautiful actress. However, when asked about how she felt about it, Paz stated that she was honored but “I’m not the new Penélope Cruz. I’m Paz Vega. There’s only one Penélope and she’s marvelous”
  • She competed in season 3 of MasterChef Celebrity in 2018. She had even cooked her way up to the finals. However, she was laid back at 2nd place and lost to the beautiful synchronized swimmer Ona Carbonell.
  • Over time, Paz has worked in various kind of films such as English, French, Italian, Mexican, and Spanish.
  • Her undying effort and hard work have earned her various honorary medals such as Medal of Andalucía, the Seville Medal, as well as gracious Seville Gold Medal.
  • She once stated that as she only turns actress when “action” is called out. However, once the word “cut” is said, she instantly becomes a normal person.
  • Paz has always wanted to play roles in English films and just be noted as a Spanish diva.

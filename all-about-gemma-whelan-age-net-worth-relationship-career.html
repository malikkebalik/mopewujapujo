All About Gemma Whelan | Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

English actress Gemma Whelan has been active in the business since 2005. She performs stand-up comedy and has made appearances in a variety of comedic shows and programs. In recognition of her sense of humor, she also won the Funny Women Variety Award in 2010.

Gemma gained notoriety as Yara Greyjoy in the HBO drama series “Game of Thrones.” She started her playing career in theatrical productions before moving on to movies and TV shows. She has also lent her voice to several animated films and video games.

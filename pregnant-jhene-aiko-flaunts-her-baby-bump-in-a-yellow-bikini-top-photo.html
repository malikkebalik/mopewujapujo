Pregnant Jhene Aiko Flaunts Her Baby Bump in a Yellow Bikini Top (Photo)


A new photo of the blossoming baby bump of pregnant Jhene Aiko was shared by the 34-year-old musician on social media.

 

Jhene, who is expecting a child with rapper Big Sean, posed in a yellow triangle bikini top and baggy denim trousers, showcasing her growing belly in a selfie.

 

Her pregnancy was recently announced with an ethereal photo shoot posted on her Instagram account.

 

 

This will be Aiko’s second child and the first for her partner, rapper Big Sean. She has a 13-year-old daughter, Namiko, with singer O’Ryan.

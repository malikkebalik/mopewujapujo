Hunter Hayes

Hunter Easton Hayes, better known as Hunter Hayes, is a well-known country music singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist from the United States. In 2011, he released his self-titled first album, which peaked at number seven on the Billboard 200 and first on the Top Country Albums, selling over 1.1 million copies. “Wanted,” the album’s most popular track, has sold over 3.5 million copies and made Hayes the youngest male act to ever reach the top of the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. He has been nominated for five Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist, and has won three BMI Awards, as well as the CMA Award for New Artist of the Year in 2012. Hayes is active on social media platforms such as Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. He has 682k Instagram followers and 1.52 million Twitter followers. He also has a Facebook following of over a million people.

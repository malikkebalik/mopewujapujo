Tragedy struck on August 5, 2023, when the news of Michael Wilcox’s passing shook the hearts of the Starke County community. Michael, a dedicated paramedic, lost his life in a car accident while bravely responding to an emergency call. The collision occurred at the intersection of County Roads 800 South and 700 East, forever changing the lives of those involved. Michael Wilcox Fatal Car Accident

The devastating accident that claimed the life of Michael Wilcox occurred at approximately 3:20 p.m. Central Time when he and fellow paramedic Brandy Salita were responding to an urgent call. Their Starke County Emergency Medical Technicians vehicle collided with a passenger car at the intersection of County Roads 800 South and 700 East. The impact of the crash left Michael Wilcox fatally injured, and Brandy Salita fighting for her life in critical condition at Northwest Hospital in La Porte.

Paying Tribute to a Fallen Hero

In an outpouring of grief and respect, first responders from Starke County came together to plan a solemn procession in honor of their fallen coworker, Michael Wilcox. The procession, held along US 35, accompanied Michael’s body as it was respectfully transported to the Coroner’s Office. This moving tribute was a testament to the profound impact Michael had on his colleagues and the community he served.

Remembering Michael Wilcox’s Legacy

Michael Wilcox will be remembered as a true hero who dedicated his life to serving others.

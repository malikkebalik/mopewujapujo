Meganne Young is a South African actress and model who is known for playing the role of Anne in the sci-fi action adventure comedy TV series, Blood Drive, and for having been cast as Rachel in the American teen romantic comedy film, The Kissing Booth. She has also been a part of various other shows and movies which include We Are Not Alone, DC’s Legends of Tomorrow, The Bull, The Dating Game Killer, Supernatural, The Challenger Disaster, The Giver, Bluestone 42, Zum Teufel mit der Wahrheit, Black Sails, Saints & Strangers, Origins: The Journey of Humankind, and Eye in the Sky. Meganne Young has also accumulated a huge social media fan base with more than 30k followers on Instagram.

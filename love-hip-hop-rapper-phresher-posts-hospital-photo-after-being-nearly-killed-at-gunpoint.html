'Love &amp; Hip Hop Rapper Phresher Posts Hospital Photo After Being Nearly Killed At Gunpoint

Phresher nearly lost his life last week when the Brooklyn rapper was robbed and beaten down at gunpoint. The Love & Hip Hop star recounted the frightening near-fatal altercation on Thursday (November 26), which made this Thanksgiving extra special to be surrounded by loved ones.

“Last Friday I put my family, friends and team through a Real Scare,” he captioned a selfie from the hospital. “I was robbed at gunpoint … gun butted and almost kidnapped. They took my car, money, jewelry but they couldn’t take my spirit.”

Phresher continued, “I’M STILL HERE and for that I am grateful to be able to tell you this because things could have been different. 2020 has been a difficult year but we gotta keep pushing. EVERY DAY YOU WAKE UP IS A GIFT … JUST OPEN IT! Remember this what don’t kill will only make you stronger.”

Phresher’s girlfriend referenced the tragic incident with a family photo post to Instagram counting her blessings on Thanksgiving.

&quot;Being famous is a curse sometimes&quot; Mofe Duncan laments after he was accused of lying that he bought



Mofe Duncan took to Instagram to lament after he was accused of lying when he said he bought a bunch of plantain for 6,000 Naira.

The actor shared the bunch of less than 4 fingers of plantain and complained that it cost him 6k.

Social media users were skeptical and questioned his claim.

 

He returned to Instagram to complain about the scrutiny he was subjected to by social media users claiming that he lied about the price of the plantain.

He added that being a celebrity is a curse sometimes as he has to filter whatever he’s posting, and even then, it still gets dissected and discussed.

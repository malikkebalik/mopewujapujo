Who is Marlo Thomas? - Marlo Thomas Wikipedia

Marlo Thomas Wikipedia: - Marlo Thomas is a famous American actress, producer, author, and social activist. She was born on November 21, 1937, in Detroit, Michigan, U.S. Thomas starred in a number of TV shows, such as The Donna Reed Show, Bonanza, My Favorite Martian, McHale's Navy, Ben Casey, and 77 Sunset Strip.

When Mike Nichols cast her in The Barefoot in the Park, it was her big break in 1965. In 1965, Thomas starred in the Two's Company pilot on ABC. After five years, Thomas decided to terminate the series in 1971. Her children's book, Free to Be... You and Me, was published in 1972. Thomas appeared as a guest on the NBC situation comedy The Practice in 1976.

Danny Thomas, Thomas's father, established St. Jude Children's Research Hospital in 1962. Thomas currently holds the position of National Outreach Director. Thomas is the winner of four Emmy Awards, a Peabody Award, a Jefferson Award, a Golden Globe Award, and a Grammy Award.

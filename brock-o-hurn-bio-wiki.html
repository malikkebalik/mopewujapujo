Brock OHurn Bio (Wiki)

Who is Brock O’Hurn?

Brock O’Hurn is an American model, actor, and social media star. He is famous for his Instagram profile.

Also, he is widely famous for his role in Too Close to Home, Boo! A Madea Halloween, and Boo 2! A Madea Halloween. Furthermore, he will be seen in Rayden Valkyrie.

Brock O’Hurn: Birth, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Brock O’Hurn was born on August 19, 1991, in California to his father, Adam Hurn, and his mother, Paige Hurn.

However, his parents separated when he was nine years old. After the divorce, he and his siblings’ custody was with his mother.

He has three siblings: Drake O’Hurn, Carly O’Hurn, and Aspyn O’Hurn. However, he hasn’t shared many of his childhood details.

Because of his mother’s job in different places, they had to keep moving to different places including Orange County, Palm Springs, and San Bernardino. Hence, he has friends from different parts of the place.

Brock O’Hurn is of Irish ancestry.

Brock O’Hurn: Professional Life, Career

Brock O’Hurn started his career installing heating and cooling systems in his uncle’s company. later, he worked in Abercrombie & Fitch and True Religion Stores.

However, he was not happy with what he was doing.

He loved working on his body, so he started training and making his physique. He then worked as a personal trainer in LA and started gaining followers on his Instagram account.

Brock O’Hurn initially gained fame as an Instagram celebrity. Because of his attractive looks and hair, he attracted many Instagram users.

As a result, he has more than 2.8M followers.

His video, In Light of Man Bun Monday and No-Shave November, Let’s Do It! on Facebook became a hit and viral, it garnered 5 million views in a very short time.

His fame landed him up to cast in Boo! A Madea Halloween in 2016. His debut came as a huge step toward his acting career. Consequently, he has appeared in Boo 2! A Madea Halloween.

Furthermore, he is the main cast of the series Too Close to Home. He has been successful in his career so there will surely be many other professional practices of his to talk about.

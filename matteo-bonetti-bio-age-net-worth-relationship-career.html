How did Matteo Bonetti start his Professional Career?

Matteo Bonetti begins working as an international soccer analyst for ESPN in August 2018, where he will cover significant international competitions. In his capacity as a studio commentator for ESPN FC and ESPN+, he imparts his expertise on European and Serie A leagues.

2008 marked the beginning of his ESPN career as a soccer journalist for ESPN.com. In 2012, he severed ways with ESPN to join beIN Sport, where he served as a presenter, color commentator, studio analyst, and play-by-play announcer.

During that time, he provided coverage of European leagues, national teams, and major sporting events, including the UEFA European Football Championship, FIFA World Cup, Africa Cup of Nations, and Copa America. Matteo has additionally served as a presenter and executive producer for the Serie A CalcioCast podcast and as a play-by-play commentator for TNT.

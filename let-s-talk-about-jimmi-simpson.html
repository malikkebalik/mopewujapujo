Let's Talk About Jimmi Simpson

Jimmi Simpson, also known as James Raymond Simpson is an American actor. He was born on 21st November 1975 and is 47 years as of 2023. 

He was born in Hackettstown, New Jersey, United States. Jimmi is known for starring in television series such as, It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia, The Late Show with David Letterman, Psych, Breakout Kings, House of Cards, Hap and Leonard, Westworld, Black Mirror, and Unsolved.

Jimmi's Education

James enrolled at Hackettstown High School, a high school in his hometown in New Jersey. The present actor attended his first acting classes. After High School, Simpson attended Bloomsburg University where he graduated with a BA in theater.

Early Life and Family

Jimmi has revealed he has two brothers and was raised by his mother but not their names. We have no source to find out about the names of his family members. 

After graduating from college Jimmi acted for four seasons at the Williamstown Theatre Festival in Williamstown, Massachusetts.

Networth

This Westworld star has amassed a sum of over $6 million over the course of his acting career, just like Dan Povenmire. After all, he has been working in the entertainment industry for over 2 decades (22 years).

Personal Life

Jimmi from the start of his career has been found to always be in another movie or show after one ends. After his roles, he gets busy with interviews, and fan servicing, and from what is seen on his socials he is a very outgoing person who's always meeting new and popular personalities. 

He also seems to be an animal lover and often posts photos of the animals being saved by "animalsasia". He has many dogs whose photos he frequently posts.

Relationships

Jimmi Simpson has been married twice in his life and is currently single. Firstly to Melanie Lynskey in 2007 and later to Sophia Del Pizzo in 2019.

Melanie Lynskey and Jimmi first met while working on the mini-series, Rose Red in 2001. They got married in 2007 and separated in the year 2012 after Lynskey filed for a divorce, thus ending their 5 years of togetherness. 

After the separation, Jimmi found English actress and comedian Sophia Del Pizzo. They acted together in Asya Nikolaeva 2020 short film Symphony, a 2019 episode of Epix Perpetual Grace, LTD, and a 2019 episode of Hulu's Into the Dark. Jimmi and Sophia eloped at an LA courthouse in April 2019 and had their nuptials at Fairmont Miramar Hotel & Bungalows in Santa Monica.

Career

Jimmi jumpstarted his career with a role in the teen comedy 2000 movie, Loser along with another role in the TV series, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation. Accompanied by various other shows and movies such as, It`s Always Sunny in Philadelphia (2005-2013), The Late Show with David Letterman (2008-2009), Psych (2009-2013), House of Cards (2014-2015), Westworld (2016-2020), Zodiac (2007), Date Night (2010), Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter (2012), White House Down (2013), Under the Silver Lake (2018), Unhinged (2020), and Breaking news in Yuba County (2021).

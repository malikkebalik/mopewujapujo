Who Is Tiffany Nguyen?

Tiffany Nguyen is a South California-born dentist and an adventure travel photographer.

She specializes in travel, adventure, landscapes, and lifestyle photography. She gets her inspiration from exploring new places and becoming immersed in foreign cultures.

Over the past few years, travel has significantly increased its importance in Tiffany's life. By documenting her travels, she intends to encourage people to step outside and plan their own adventures.

Working with numerous businesses and tourism organizations, such as Sony, BMW, Amazon, United Airlines, Destination Canada, and Apple, Tiffany has developed a diversified portfolio.

The Huffington Post, Travel + Leisure, USA Today, Mashable, Cosmopolitan, Buzzfeed, Reddit, and other publications have all published Tiffany's work.

We can also follow her on Instagram to see her amazing works. 

Assuming from her social media, she lives quite a comfortable life. She is endorsed and sponsored by several brands about which we have talked below.

The typical travel photographer in the US earns $22,066 per year, according to Comparably. San Francisco has the highest average total remuneration for travel photographers at $29,959, which is 36% higher than the US average.

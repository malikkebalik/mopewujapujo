Jay R. Ferguson Net Worth

Who is Jay R. Ferguson?

Jay R. Ferguson is an American actor born in Dallas, Texas in July 1974. He has a net worth of $3 million. Ferguson has appeared in several movies and TV shows. He starred as Ponyboy Curtis in The Outsiders in 1990 and as Taylor Newton in Evening Shade from 1990 to 1994. He has also starred in movies such as Higher Learning, The Lucky One, and The Killer Inside Me.

Ferguson has been nominated for a Screen Actors Guild Award and three Young Artist Awards. Since 2010, he has played the role of Stan Rizzo in the TV series Mad Men. Ferguson has also appeared in TV series such as Demontown, Judging Amy, Surface, Sleeper Cell, and Easy Money.

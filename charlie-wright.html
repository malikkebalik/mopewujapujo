Charlie Wright- Professional Career

Charlie attempted to become a model before entering into acting, but after failing badly, he quickly went to acting. Only a year later, in 2017, he made his film debut as Chuck in “Ingrid Goes West,” a comedy-drama starring Aubrey Plaza, Elizabeth Olsen, and O’Shea Jackson Jr. The film was a commercial success, earning multiple prizes and advancing Charlie’s career.

Charlie’s most prominent film to date was released a few months later; he played Rodrick Heffley in “Diary of a Wimpy Kid: The Long Haul,” the fourth entry in the enormously successful “Wimpy Kid” film franchise. For the first three parts, the cast was the same, but for the fourth, producers and directors chose to switch the cast, which raised various questions from the public and reviewers.

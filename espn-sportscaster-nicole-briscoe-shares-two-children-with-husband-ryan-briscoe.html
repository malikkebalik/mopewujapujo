ESPN Sportscaster, Nicole Briscoe Shares Two Children With Husband, Ryan Briscoe

Nicole Briscoe is an American sportscaster currently serving for the ESPN. Briscoe is mostly famous for covering NASCAR and racing events.

Briscoe was born as Nicole Mankse on 2nd July 1980, in Wausau, Wisconsin, United States of America. Born to her American parents, she is of White ethnicity and holds an American nationality. 

Married to an Australian Racer

The famous sportscaster and host is happy with her married life. In 2009, Nicole exchanged her wedding vows with Ryan Briscoe, who is a renowned car racer. 

Nicole and her husband share two children from their 11 years of a marital relationship. Both of them are daughters named Blake James Briscoe (born in 2016) and Finley Briscoe (born in 2013). 

Since the marriage, Nicole is living happily with her husband and children, as seen on their social media. Recently, Briscoe uploaded a picture with her family and friends enjoying skiing. 

Winner of the Miss Illinois Teen USA 1998

Soon after graduating from the Hononegah High School, Nicole participated in the Miss Illinois Teen USA 1998 and won the contest. 

After two years, Nicole again took part in the Miss Illinois USA and passed her title to Rebecca Ambrosi as she ended up being the first runner up. 

Kicked off her career as a News Reporter

A few years later, Briscoe graduated from Northern Illinois University and joined the WREX-TV, by NBC station in Rockford. Then, she also worked as the general assignment reporter for WANE-TV in Indiana. 

In 2004, Briscoe also covered the Indianapolis 500-Mile Race, the NFL's Indianapolis Colts, and the United States Grand Prix. In 2006, Nicole served as the co-host of The speed Report, as she replaced Connie LeGrand. 

Currently working as the Sports Anchor

After working at The Speed Record for two years, Nicole started hosting NASCAR events via ESPN/ABC in 2008, where Lindsay Czarniak also served as the sportscaster. The host worked for almost six years and left the show in 2014. 

The following year, Nicole joined SportsCenter, a daily sports news television program where she serves as an anchor. 

How much Net Worth does Nicole accumulate?

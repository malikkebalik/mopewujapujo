[PICS] See Southern Charm Star Cameran Eubanks Family Hollywood Life

Cameran Eubanks is not new to the television scene! The VERY smart blonde has starred in ‘MTV’s Real World: San Diego,’ ‘The Challenge,’ and ‘Watch What Happens Live!’ She then went on to make it big in Hollywood with her appearance on ‘Southern Charm’ in 2014. This was a docu series that followed wealthy families in Charleston, S.C. as they dealt with all the money, scandal, and romance in their lives all while trying to preserve their family names! On the show we saw Eubanks embark on a journey into motherhood and balance her career in real estate, facing all the challenges of a working mom! We love to see it!

Today she has a beautiful family consisting of hubby Jason Wimberly and 2-year-old daughter Palmer Corrine Wiberly. 

Plus, she’s got killer style, and we would honestly love some fashion tips, as well as financial tips from this guru!

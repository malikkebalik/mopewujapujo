Bio of Alejandro Salomon

Born on 7 December 1984, in Mexico City, Mexico, Alejandro Salomon is a well-known YouTube personality and entrepreneur. He is most recognized for his YouTube channel “Salomondrin,” which primarily focuses on supercars. Over the years, he has also achieved success in the financial and real estate industries, contributing to his current net worth.

As of late-2017, Salomon’s net worth is estimated to be $25 million, garnered through a variety of successful ventures, and it is anticipated to rise as he continues his career.

Alejandro Salomon (Salomondrin) Net Worth: $25 million

Salomondrin’s journey into wealth began in Mexico, where he later moved to the US with his closest friend. His pursuit of his passion for film led to the establishment of Helios Productions. Serving as a producer for various films, such as “Slightly Single in LA”, “Triple Dog”, “Zoe Gone”, and “Raised by Wolves”, has significantly contributed to his net worth. Additionally, his appearance in some of these films, including “Pocket Listing”, “One Day Like Rain”, and “Martial Science”, has further bolstered his financial standing.

He subsequently ventured into television movies and series, producing shows like “Bangin’ Gears” and appearing in “Rocky: The Final Battle”. Apart from his entertainment endeavors, he established an investment company called Salomon Investment Partners and ventured into the real estate sector, adding to his wealth.

In 2014, Salomondrin created his YouTube channel, exclusively dedicated to his love for supercars. The success in his other ventures has facilitated funding for the channel’s content. With over 800,000 subscribers and millions of views on his videos, his net worth and popularity have significantly grown in just a few years. He frequently updates his channel with new content, while also managing various businesses, including those in technology, entertainment, and dairy. His recent television projects include “Pretty Little Dead Girl” and “Secrets of My Stepdaughter”, illustrating the continuous rise of his net worth.

On the personal front, Salomondrin married Bele Maria in 2014 and resides in Los Angeles, California, where he continues to work on films. He actively maintains a personal website, selling his content and merchandise, and engages with a large following on various social media platforms.
